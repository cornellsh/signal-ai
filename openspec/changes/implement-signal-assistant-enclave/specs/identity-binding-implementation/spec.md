## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Secure Identity Binding
#### Scenario: Secure Identity Binding
The enclave SHALL securely bind user identities to enclave-generated keys or data.

### Requirement: Identity Verification
#### Scenario: Identity Verification
The enclave SHALL provide mechanisms to verify bound identities.
