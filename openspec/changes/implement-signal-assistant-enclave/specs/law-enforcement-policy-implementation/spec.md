## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Policy Enforcement
#### Scenario: Policy Enforcement
The enclave SHALL enforce defined law enforcement access policies, ensuring data access only under specified conditions.

### Requirement: Auditability of Access
#### Scenario: Auditability of Access
The enclave SHALL log all instances of law enforcement data access for auditability.
